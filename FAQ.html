FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are your shipping times?

+

We typically process and ship orders within 1-2 business days. Domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days, while international shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return window for all unworn items with original tags. Returns are free for domestic orders. International returns are subject to shipping costs. Items must be in their original packaging.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly through the shipping carrier's website.

What happens if my product arrives damaged?

+

If your product arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We'll provide a prepaid return label and send you a replacement item at no additional cost.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs vary by location and are calculated at checkout. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
